Banana Bread

Put those ripe bananas to use in the best banana bread recipe ever. Moist and delicious, it's easy to makeâ€”one bowl, no need for a mixer!

Ingredients

  • 3 medium very ripe bananas
  • 1/3 up butter, melted
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1.5 cups flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 deg. F.
  2. Butter an 8x4 inch loaf pan.
  3. Mash the bananas and add the butter.
  4. Mix in the remaining ingredients.
  5. Pour into loaf pan.
  6. Bake for 55 to 65 minutes.
